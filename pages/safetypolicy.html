Safety Policy

It is NEKAD Policy to conduct its business in a manner that protects the safety of employees, others involved in its operations, customers public. NEKAD will strive to prevent all accidents, injuries and occupational illnesses through the active participation of every employee. NEKAD is committed to continuous effort to identify and eliminate or manage safety risks associated with its activities.

In furtherance of this policy NEKAD will:-

  • Design facilities, establish practices and provide training in a manner that safeguards workers, property and communities in which its works.
  • Respond quickly, effectively and with care to emergencies or accidents resulting from its works, cooperating with industrial organizations and authorized government agencies.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations and apply responsible standards of its own where laws and regulations do not exist.
  • Conduct or support research to extend knowledge about the safety effects of its job, promptly applying significant finding and sharing them with employee, customers, industry, government agencies and the public.
  • Stress to all employees their responsibility and accountability for safety performance.
  • Undertake appropriate reviews and evaluations of its works to measure progress and to ensure compliance with this safety policy
