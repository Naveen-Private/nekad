Who We Are

NEKAD DEVELOPMENT ENGINEERING SDN BHD (NEKAD) is wholly owned and operated by Bumiputra established in January 1995. NEKAD has an operational office and yard located at PT 11616, Taman Industri Paka, Kg Chachar Baru, Mukim Kuala Paka, 23100 Paka, Dungun, Terengganu Darul Iman.

Core Values

We practice the highest standard of business ethics and integrity, promote interaction and co-operation at all level of staffs, customers and business associates. Practicing towards achieving the HSE standards, rules and regulation as per client requirement and continuously to seek way to improve quality, productivity and cost efficiency.

Our Mission

To be a preferred Service Provider to our customers through the continuous skilled and competency learning by our personnel to be a knowledgeable workforce to continue searching for new methods and technology in keeping pace with the fast changing and demanding business environment.

Our Vision

To be a world class Contractor in supporting the plant operation by delivering effective solutions to plant’s integrity and reliability requirement. Ensure consistent quality of services and products to comply with customers’ needs and requirement.
